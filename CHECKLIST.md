## üêõ Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

### Profile not showing?
- Wait 2-3 minutes after push
- Verify repository name = username exactly
- Check repository is public

### Logo not animating?
- Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R / Ctrl+F5
- Wait 5-10 minutes for cache update
- Verify you uploaded `jh_logo.svg` (not static version)

### Stats not updating?
- Check username spelling in all URLs
- Wait 10-15 minutes for initial load
- Verify you have public repositories

### Badges not rendering?
- Check internet connection
- Verify no special characters breaking URLs
- Wait a few minutes and refresh

### Typewriter not working?
- Check service is online
- Verify no special characters in text
- Try refresh after a few minutes
